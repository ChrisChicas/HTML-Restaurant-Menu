Origin Story

Established back in 1974 at our flagship location in Edgewater, the original owner; Rel Namio; had a vision. A vision to spread his family's homemade recipies to one and all, and to continue to perfect his craft as he continued this path. Eventually, the people in the area began to rave about the food's "heavenly" quality, giving rise to the restaurant's catchphrase to date!
